About Corevexis

Corevexis is a modern, cloud-first semiconductor design platform offering professional tools for IC design, SPICE simulation, digital verification, and collaborative EDA workflows.

Mission

Make semiconductor design accessible, collaborative, and scalable by delivering powerful EDA tooling in the cloud.

Vision

Enable teams of all sizes to design world-class silicon with cloud-native workflows and industry-grade toolchains.

Product Overview

  • Cloud SPICE simulation with high-performance backends
  • Digital logic design and verification
  • Real-time collaborative editing and versioning
  • Industry integrations and export-ready flows

Roadmap (2026)

  1. Q1: Multi-user collaboration, shared projects
  2. Q2: Improved SPICE performance and cloud batches
  3. Q3: New synthesis and timing analysis tools
  4. Q4: Enterprise integrations and advanced security
